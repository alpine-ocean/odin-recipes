Tuna Noodles

Description

The addition of sardines really elevates the profile of this dish. It's acidity is front and center but doesn't crowd the stage. The only downside of this dish is the amount of water it consumes.

Ingredients

  • 3.5 oz spaghetti
  • 1 can tuna in oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 anchovy can
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tsp capers
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta w/ salt; scoop out ~1 cup pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta
  2. In the same pot, heat the reserved oil from the tuna cans over medium heat. Add the garlic, anchovies and chilli flakes. Cook and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add the pasta, capers and 1/3 cup of reserved pasta water. Toss using 2 spatulas for 2 minutes.
