Indian Naan

Description

This is ridiculously good boat-made Indian naan that we make on our sailboat. It's really easy to make from scratch, from simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup + 1 Tbsp warm water
  • 1 1/2 tsp maple syrup
  • Olive oil (for greasing bowl and frying)
  • butter & cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine flour, yeast, salt, warm water, and maple syrup in a bowl - mix thoroughly
  2. Cover and let rise for 1 1/2 hours
  3. Divide and shape, then let rise for an additional 15-20 minutes
  4. Fry in pan over med-high heat for 1-2 minutes on each side
  5. Butter both sides and garnish with cilantro before serving
